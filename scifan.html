About this model

A ResNet-based image classifier trained with fastai on images scraped from the web — sci-fi imagery (spaceships, robots, futuristic cities) vs. fantasy imagery (dragons, magic, medieval landscapes). Built as part of Practical Deep Learning for Coders (Lesson 1). Hosted on Hugging Face Spaces.
